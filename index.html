Brief Company Background

BATKINS INCORPORATED was registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission on February 3, 2004. Although young, the company has roots established in 1988 and has assumed all warranties of CONSPROC PHILIPPINES, INC. BATKINS specializes in waterproofing, grouting, floor hardening, sealants, epoxy injection, structural retrofitting, environmental protection, soil stabilization, and the manufacturing of selected construction chemicals and building maintenance products. The company continues to serve long-term clients in the construction and building maintenance industry, and its senior officers have more than 40 years of experience. BATKINS is committed to providing reliable service to its core clients wherever they execute projects in the Philippines.
